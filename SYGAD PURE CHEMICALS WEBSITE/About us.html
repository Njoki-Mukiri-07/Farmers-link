About Farmers Link Feeds

Farmers Link Feeds is a trusted agrovet dedicated to supporting farmers with high-quality animal feeds, veterinary products, and expert-backed solutions for modern farming. We serve livestock farmers across dairy, poultry, goats, sheep, and pigs, helping them achieve healthier animals and better productivity. At Farmers Link Feeds, we focus on the science behind animal health. Our product range includes balanced nutrition feeds, trace minerals, vitamins, probiotics, and performance-enhancing supplements carefully selected to promote efficient growth, strong immunity, and consistent farm output. We proudly partner with Syngad Pure Chemicals, a manufacturer established in 1985, to supply reliable and scientifically formulated animal health products. This partnership allows us to provide farmers with trusted, high-quality solutions that meet modern agricultural standards. Beyond products, we are committed to empowering farmers with knowledge and practical support. We guide our customers on proper feeding programs, disease prevention, and livestock management practices to ensure maximum returns on their investment.

Our Manufacturing Partner

At Farmers Link, we believe that true quality starts long before the product reaches the farm. This is why we have integrated our production through our very own Sygad Pure Chemical, a specialized facility dedicated exclusively to the science of animal health.

By overseeing the manufacturing process ourselves, we ensure every formulation is crafted with clinical precision and a deep focus on nutrition. This direct partnership doesn't just guarantee superior quality-controlled products; it ensures that we maintain a reliable and consistent supply chain that you can depend on, season after season, to keep your livestock thriving.
